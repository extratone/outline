Puma made Sonic the Hedgehog shoes and they’re totally insane

Joshua Topolsky

Have you ever wanted a shoe that looked just like it was made out of the physical materials present in the video game Sonic The Hedgehog? Well guess what — Puma has you covered. The new models, based on Puma’s RS-0 sneaker, will come in the furry blue “Sonic” version, as well as the cartoonishly sexy, red leather “Dr. Eggman” model.

I honestly can’t tell if these are gross or cool, but I’m buying them anyway. High Snobiety reports that the duo will be available June 5th for $130 from Puma and select retailers. No word on availabilty for the Game Gear pictured in these press photos.
