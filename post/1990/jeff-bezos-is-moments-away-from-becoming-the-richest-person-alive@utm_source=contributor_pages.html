Jeff Bezos is moments away from becoming the richest person alive

It turns out opening a bookstore is pretty good business after all.

Joshua Topolsky Jul—27—2017 08:00AM EST

Jeff Bezos is known as a man who loves to laugh. Soon he’ll be laughing... all the way... to the bank. It’s likely that in a matter of days (or weeks), the Amazon founder’s net worth will surpass that of Bill Gates, making him the richest person alive. A mere $900 million stands between Bezos and the ultimate prize in this world: having the most money.

Currently Bezos’ number stands at $89.3 billion after a $1 billion windfall on Wednesday. His overall net worth gained $2.2 billion this week alone thanks to a sweet 2 percent bump in Amazon’s stock. Bezos officially became the second richest person in the world in June just after Amazon's plan to acquire Whole Foods was announced. It’s likely that the next substantial increase in the company’s share price will allow the humble bookseller to breathe the rarefied air only Bill Gates enjoys. Maybe to celebrate he’ll buy another newspaper.

Update: He did it! Bloomberg reports that Bezos, who was the fourth-richest person in the world at the beginning of the year, is now the wealthiest human being on the planet.
