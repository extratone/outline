Joshua Topolsky May—24—2017 06:38PM EST

According to a report from CNN, Attorney General Jeff Sessions failed to disclose contact with Russian officials on a form required for security clearance. Sessions didn't mention two meetings with Russian Ambassador Sergey Kislyak on the form, which requires applicants to list “any contact” they've had with a foreign government or its representatives in the past seven years. Oops.
