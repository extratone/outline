People are losing Social Security pay because of federal student loan debt

A government report showed 114,000 people over age 50 losing part of their benefits after defaulting on student loans.
The report also found the number of people over age 65 facing this problem has increased 540 percent over the last decade.
In addition to withholding Social Security benefits, the government can garnish wages and withhold tax returns.
