Every Monday through Thursday, The Outline World Dispatch brings you stories about Power, Culture, and the Future. We guarantee you’ll feel smarter and more hydrated. Hosted by yours truly.

Outline staff writer Gaby Del Valle had a simple question when she went to the March For Our Lives demonstration in D.C. this past weekend: Why are you here today? The answers she got were varied, but with a unifying thread. Young people spoke about living in fear of gun violence in their schools, and many people she spoke with were impacted by gun violence in some way. Today’s episode is full of the voices she heard.
