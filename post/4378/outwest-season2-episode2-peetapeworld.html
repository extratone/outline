Out West Podcast Season 2, Episode 2: Pee Tape World

The second episode of Westworld's second season puts hosts at odds but maybe explains the true purpose of the park.

Outline Staff May—01—2018 10:10AM EST

The first episode of the second season of Westworld introduced a couple of new timelines; the second episode introduced several. Some questions seem answered, like where Westworld is or what purpose it has beyond sheer entertainment; for others, the mythology remains cryptic.

In this episode, Casey, Josh, and guest host Paris dissect all the possibilities that flow from the multitude of plot points and pregnant, vague statements that constitute episode two. Many things are possible, but only a few are plausible.

Reminder: Out West considers the fan theory a form of high art and a kind of religion. If you've got your own, send them to us as a voice memo to outwest@theoutline.com or find us right here at Anchor.
