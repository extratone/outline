Out West Podcast, Season 2 Episode 7: Ghost Nation, Woke Nation

“You and the hosts are something different. An original work. More just, more noble.”

Outline Staff Jun—05—2018 02:42PM EST

It’s been a long week. So long, in fact, that some of our esteemed podcasters fell asleep when watching the newest episode of Westworld. Does this hamper his (obviously, it wasn’t Casey) ability to guess what might happen in this famously discursive show? Does a human-host hybrid maintain fidelity after a few days? The answer, dear listener, is no.

In this episode, Casey, Josh, Jeremy, and Paris discuss how William can survive so many gunshots, the finer points of the Mission Impossible series, Casey’s secret Reddit account, and much more.

Reminder: Out West considers the fan theory a form of high art and a kind of religion. If you've got your own, send them to us as a voice memo to outwest@theoutline.com or find us right here at Anchor.
