Predictive policing

“It's just not worth it.”

Oakland police decided not to use PredPol, software that predicts when and where crime will happen.
PredPol is used in Los Angeles and other major cities, but some cops say it leads to racial bias and erodes trust.
Other departments in the US have been cutting ties with PredPol.
