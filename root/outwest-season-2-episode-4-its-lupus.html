Out West Podcast, Season 2 Episode 4: It’s lupus!

And other maladies, explained.

Outline Staff May—15—2018 03:09PM EST

The fourth episode of Westworld was a massive one, in which many long-running fan theories were confirmed, and so many others were teased we began to think we were watching Lost. Also, we glimpsed the ideal male body, in all its muscled, dickless glory.

In this episode, Casey, Jeremy, and Josh talk about rare diseases, how right Josh is, Asian culture, Philadelphia, Aphex Twin, and much more. They all got very drunk.
