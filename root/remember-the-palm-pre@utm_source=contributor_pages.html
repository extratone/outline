Tomorrow Podcast: Remember the Palm Pre?

We’ve been using the sadly forgotten smartphone, and we’re taking your calls about it.

Joshua Topolsky and Ryan Houlihan Nov—06—2017 11:32AM EST

The Palm Pre, a 2009 smartphone made by Palm, was ahead of its time. It ran webOS, an operating system that incorporated swipe gestures, cards, global search; basically, it introduced a lot of ideas about phones that we take for granted today.

Palm and WebOS are long gone now, but this week on the Tomorrow podcast, we’re dusting off our Pres, lighting them up, and taking your calls live about what's changed in phones since these were made.
