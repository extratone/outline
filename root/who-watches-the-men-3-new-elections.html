Who watches the men? #3: New elections

Part three of a Trump-era comic inspired by ‘Watchmen.’

Jeremy Gordon and Reno Maniquis Apr—11—2018 02:15PM EST

Last year, we launched Who Watches the Men, a comic about Trump’s America styled after Alan Moore and Dave Gibbons’ iconic Watchmen series. After a long hiatus, we’re bringing the comic back, with a new installment coming every other week. We pick up in the 12th year of Trump’s presidency, and finally, new elections are being held. The forces seeking to shape the future of this country gather in silence, waiting for their moment...
