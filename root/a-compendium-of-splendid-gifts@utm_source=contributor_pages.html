We take the holidays very seriously at The Outline. We love gift giving. Our hearts are open and filled with joy. Our smiles signal what our hearts are feeling. The feeling is a sensation about the season. The season is a time for giving and sharing. Sharing means opening your heart and also your wallet. Your wallet is a pathway to joy. Joy is another word for happy. A gift is another way to say I love you. Love is another gift you can give. But you can't giftwrap love. You have to giftwrap a gift. That's why we made these guides for you. Spend time with your loved ones this holiday season, and also spend money on your loved ones.
