Man, what happened to the ‘Copy Link to Tweet’ button?

He’s not gone, he just moved.

Paris Martineau

Remember this l’il guy? You'd tap on him to copy the link to some (probably awful) tweet so you could share it with your friends?

Now, when you tap on that little caret shaped dude, you get this irrelevant list of options.

Gone, but not forgotten. His memory lives on in arrow-pointing-out-of-a-tray button on the bottom right. If you click “share tweet via,” the sharing options will display. The Outline Editor-in-Chief Josh Topolsky notes that the share button is still in the main menu on the Android version of the app because “Android is better at everything.”
