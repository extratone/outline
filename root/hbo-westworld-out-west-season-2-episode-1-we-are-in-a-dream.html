Out West Podcast, Season 2 Episode 1: We are in a dream

The first episode of Westworld’s second season may answer a couple of questions, but it asks far more.

Outline Staff Apr—25—2018 09:25AM EST

Westworld season 2 has officially begun, and with it, a new sea of questions has arisen. The mysteries this season seem somehow deeper. They beg the viewer to plunge into them, to cast away old theories and reconsider their previously held notions.

And like a cold splash of water on a sleepy face, Casey, Josh, and Jeremy deliver answers to the puzzles swirling in your mind. Is Bernard being used? Is Dolores really in control? How does the Man in Black stay so spry given his age? Dive deep into the first Out West analysis, but please don’t forget to pack your spacesuit.

Reminder: Out West considers the fan theory a form of high art and a kind of religion. If you've got your own, send them to us as a voice memo to outwest@theoutline.com or find us right here at Anchor.

And remember: Hell is empty and all the devils are here.
