Michigan signs comprehensive self-driving regulations into law

Michigan joins Ohio and Pennsylvania as a testing ground for new self-driving technology.
The law will require carmakers to take full liability for accidents in which the responsible vehicle was driving itself
The employment implications for autonomous vehicles could be staggering. Some estimate that over 4 million jobs could be at risk.
